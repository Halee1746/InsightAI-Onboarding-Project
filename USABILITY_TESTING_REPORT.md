# USABILITY TESTING REPORT

## Objective

Evaluate the usability and effectiveness of the InsightAI onboarding experience and identify opportunities for improvement.

---

## Participants

| Participant | Background |
|------------|------------|
| User 1 | Student |
| User 2 | Marketing Professional |
| User 3 | Startup Founder |
| User 4 | UI/UX Designer |
| User 5 | Data Analyst |

---

## Testing Tasks

1. Create an account.
2. Complete the onboarding process.
3. Connect a data source.
4. Create the first analytics report.
5. Reach the success screen.

---

## Findings

| Finding | Severity |
|----------|----------|
| Progress indicator was not immediately noticeable | High |
| Goal selection required clearer descriptions | Medium |
| Users expected a skip option during setup | Low |

---

## Improvements Implemented

### Improvement 1
Before:
- Small progress indicator

After:
- Larger progress bar with clear step visibility

---

### Improvement 2
Before:
- Generic goal selection options

After:
- Added descriptive goal cards with supporting text

---

### Improvement 3
Before:
- Data source section appeared crowded

After:
- Improved spacing and visual hierarchy

---

## Overall Results

Most participants successfully completed onboarding and reached the activation stage without assistance.

Users reported that the onboarding process felt clear, structured, and easy to follow.

---

## Conclusion

The final onboarding experience effectively reduces onboarding friction and helps users achieve their first value moment efficiently.
